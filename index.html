Who we are:

We are the Official University of Manitoba Undergraduate Statistics Club. We are for both students in Statistics and those with an interest in Statistics.


What we do:

We host academic and social events throughout the year with the goal of connecting our members. The events include first year exam crams!


Why should you join?

It's a great way to get connected with other individuals in the department including instructors. It's free to join and you don't need to be a Stats major to join.
